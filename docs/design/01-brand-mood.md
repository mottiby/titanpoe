# 01 · Brand & Mood

## Essence
A **premium, high-trust marketplace for Path of Exile 2 services** — where hardcore players buy time,
power and expertise safely. It should feel like a **specialist guild**, not a generic shop: it knows the
game, speaks the language (leagues, Atlas, pinnacle bosses, Divine Orbs), and protects every deal.

Two influences, fused:
- **overgear.com** — clarity, conversion, and trust: prominent prices, ratings, "100% money-back / escrow"
  guarantees, ID-verified sellers, clean catalog cards, a confident primary CTA on every screen.
- **Path of Exile 2** — dark fantasy: gritty, moody, arcane. Near-black canvases, metal and ember accents,
  restrained ornamentation, a sense of depth and craft.

## Who it's for
- **Buyers** — time-poor or skill-gated players who want results fast and safely (no scams).
- **Sellers** — skilled players / teams monetizing their craft; need to look credible and get paid.
- **Moderators/owner** — need clarity and control.

## What it must make people feel
| Feeling | Why it matters | How design delivers |
|---|---|---|
| **Safe** | money + game accounts at stake | escrow/guarantee badges, status clarity, verified sellers, no dark patterns |
| **Premium** | justifies the 15% take rate | dark refined palette, generous spacing, crafted details, smooth motion |
| **In-the-know** | nichе beats the "shop-of-everything" | PoE2-specific filters/terms, league context, expert copy |
| **Fast & clear** | conversion | one obvious CTA per page, scannable cards, prices that pop |

## Design principles
1. **Dark, but legible.** Deep backgrounds, high-contrast text, never muddy. Contrast ≥ WCAG AA.
2. **Trust is visual.** Escrow, guarantee, ratings, verification and price are first-class UI, not footnotes.
3. **One job per screen.** A single, unmistakable primary action; everything else is secondary/quiet.
4. **Value glows.** Money/price/currency use the warm "value" accent (amber/gold) — the eye finds it instantly.
5. **Game-native, restrained.** Arcane mood via color, depth, and a touch of texture — not cluttered fantasy kitsch.
6. **Calm motion.** Subtle, purposeful transitions (hover lift, status changes); honor `prefers-reduced-motion`.
7. **Bilingual by construction.** Layouts breathe so longer RU strings wrap gracefully; no fixed-width labels.

## Voice & tone
- **Confident, concise, expert.** "Escrow-protected. Pay only when you confirm." Not hypey, not corporate.
- **Player-to-player**, uses the game's vocabulary correctly (EN and RU).
- Microcopy reassures at moments of risk (payment, sharing details, disputes).

## Moodboard keywords
arcane • obsidian • ember/gold • dark fantasy • guild • escrow vault • crafted • premium • focused •
trust • neon-violet glow • subtle grain • depth

## Anti-goals (do NOT do)
- ❌ The default light "AI SaaS dashboard" look.
- ❌ Bright/flat/cartoonish or rainbow gradients.
- ❌ Fantasy kitsch (dragons, scrolls, parchment textures everywhere).
- ❌ Cramped cards, tiny low-contrast text, hidden prices, or aggressive dark patterns.
